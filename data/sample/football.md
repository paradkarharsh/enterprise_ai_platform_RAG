# Football High-Level Statistics and Facts

## Lionel Messi
- **Full Name**: Lionel Andrés Messi
- **Role**: Forward, Captain of the Argentina National Team
- **2022 World Cup Achievement**:
  - Led Argentina to victory in the **2022 FIFA World Cup**.
  - Won the Golden Ball (best player) for the second time in his career.
- **Total Goals**: Over 820 career goals for club and country.
- **Awards**: Won a record 8 Ballon d'Or awards.
- **GOAT Status**: Widely considered one of the greatest players of all time (GOAT) due to his unparalleled dribbling, playmaking, and goal-scoring abilities.

## Cristiano Ronaldo
- **Full Name**: Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro
- **Role**: Forward, Captain of the Portugal National Team
- **Total Goals**: Over 870 career goals, the highest goal scorer in the history of men's football.
- **Awards**: Won 5 Ballon d'Or awards and numerous Champions League titles.
- **GOAT Status**: Frequently debated alongside Messi as the greatest of all time, known for his incredible athleticism, goal-scoring prowess, and longevity in the sport.

## Pelé
- **Total Goals**: Over 1,200 goals according to some records (757 official goals).
- **World Cup Achievements**: Only player to win three FIFA World Cups (1958, 1962, 1970).
- **GOAT Status**: One of the most famous and celebrated sports figures of the 20th century, often termed the original GOAT of football.
