# Cricket High-Level Statistics and Facts

## MS Dhoni
- **Full Name**: Mahendra Singh Dhoni
- **Role**: Wicketkeeper-Batsman, Former Captain of the Indian National Team
- **Captaincy Achievements**:
  - Led India to victory in the **2007 ICC World Twenty20**.
  - Led India to victory in the **2011 ICC Cricket World Cup**, hitting the winning six in the final against Sri Lanka.
  - Led India to victory in the **2013 ICC Champions Trophy**.
  - Only captain in the history of cricket to win all three ICC white-ball trophies.
- **Total Runs**: 
  - ODIs: 10,773 runs at an average of 50.57.
  - Tests: 4,876 runs.
- **GOAT Status**: Widely regarded as one of the greatest captains and finishers in the history of ODI cricket, and among the best wicketkeepers.

## Virat Kohli
- **Role**: Top-order Batsman, Former Captain of the Indian National Team
- **Total Runs**: 
  - ODIs: Over 13,000 runs, holding the record for the most ODI centuries (50).
  - Tests: Over 8,000 runs.
- **GOAT Status**: Considered by many as one of the modern-day "Greatest of All Time" (GOAT) in all formats of the game due to his exceptional consistency and run-scoring records.

## Sachin Tendulkar
- **Total Runs**: 
  - ODIs: 18,426 runs (highest in ODI history).
  - Tests: 15,921 runs (highest in Test history).
- **Centuries**: Only player to score 100 international centuries (51 in Tests, 49 in ODIs).
- **GOAT Status**: Widely acknowledged as the greatest batsman in the history of cricket, often referred to as the "God of Cricket".
